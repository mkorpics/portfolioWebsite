Ancient History

From the beginning, my homeschooling background set me up for success, cultivating my insatiable thirst for knowledge and learning.

Hallmarks of these times include creating my own Civil War history class, traveling to Civil War battle sites up and down the eastern coast and training in Speech and Debate.

Education

At Hillsdale College, a rigorous liberal arts school, my English and German majors fostered my love for literature and language.

Meanwhile, my passion for military history continued to thrive, most notably through classes taught by Victor Davis Hanson.

Intervening Years

My post-college years proved invaluable, forging me into the person I am today.

Over three years, my experiences included working at a guest ranch, training horse and teaching at a middle school.

My daily tasks demanded leadership, learning, patience, communication, teamwork, grit, creative problem-solving, flexibility and commitment.

Road to Code

Despite enjoying my work, I found myself unsatisfied with a life that lacked more academic, intellectual challenges. While exploring possible alternatives, experiments with coding online quickly revealed that programming suited me perfectly.

Proactive Pursuit

I quickly took initiative to figure out how to best enter a career as a programmer. After exploring various options, I decided on We Can Code IT (WCCI), a programming bootcamp.

WWCI allowed me to developed strong technical knowledge through in-depth training and hands-on experience.

Passion for Programming

Stimulating, intellectually demanding and utterly addicting, computer programming provides endless opportunities for continued learning, growth and achievement. Dedicated to performing to the best of my ability -- and with extensive experiences in teamwork and communication -- I am confident in my ability to further the interests of my future teammates and employers.

With my lifelong passion for military history and attracted by the unique opportunities in the security field, I intend to pursue a career in cyber security.
